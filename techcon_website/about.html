Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup for local tech enthusiasts and has since grown into a global event attracting developers, entrepreneurs, and thought leaders from across the world. Each year, TechCon has pushed the boundaries of innovation, fostering a community centered around learning and collaboration.

Our Mission

TechCon is dedicated to empowering the next generation of technology professionals through inclusive, insightful, and forward-thinking programming. We aim to make emerging tech accessible and create a platform where innovation meets inspiration.
